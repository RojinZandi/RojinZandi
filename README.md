### Hi, I'm Rojin Zandi

I'm a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at [Northeastern University](https://www.northeastern.edu/), researching **multimodal sensing**, **WiFi-based perception**, and **deep learning for activity recognition**.

My work focuses on fusing WiFi Channel State Information (CSI) with other sensing modalities using transformer and attention-based architectures for robust, non-intrusive recognition of human and robotic activities. I've contributed to open multimodal datasets and developed novel deep learning methods for cross-modal fusion.

---

#### Research Interests

- WiFi/RF-based sensing and perception
- Multimodal and cross-modal deep learning
- Transformer and attention architectures for signal processing
- Robotic activity recognition
- Dataset curation and benchmark design
